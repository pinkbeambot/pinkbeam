Executive Presentation Template
=================================

Professional PowerPoint template for presenting digital transformation initiatives.

SLIDE STRUCTURE:

1. Title Slide
   - Initiative name
   - Presenter name and date
   - Confidentiality notice

2. Executive Summary (1 slide)
   - Key recommendation
   - Investment required
   - Expected ROI
   - Timeline overview

3. Business Context (1-2 slides)
   - Market trends
   - Competitive landscape
   - Current challenges
   - Strategic imperatives

4. Proposed Solution (2-3 slides)
   - Solution overview
   - Key capabilities
   - Technical approach
   - Integration points

5. Implementation Roadmap (1-2 slides)
   - Phase breakdown
   - Key milestones
   - Dependencies
   - Risk mitigation

6. Investment Summary (1-2 slides)
   - Total cost breakdown
   - Funding approach
   - Cash flow projection
   - Alternative scenarios

7. Benefits & ROI (1-2 slides)
   - Quantifiable benefits
   - Strategic benefits
   - Risk-adjusted ROI
   - Payback period

8. Next Steps (1 slide)
   - Immediate actions
   - Decision required
   - Timeline for approval
   - Resources needed

DESIGN NOTES:
- Clean, professional design
- Brand-compliant colors
- Data visualization templates
- Icon library included
- Animation suggestions

TIPS FOR SUCCESS:
□ Lead with the recommendation
□ Keep to 15-20 slides maximum
□ Use data to support claims
□ Anticipate objections
□ Practice delivery
□ Prepare appendix with details
