Digital Transformation Readiness Checklist
==========================================

Assess your organization's readiness across 8 key dimensions:

LEADERSHIP
□ Executive sponsorship secured
□ Clear vision communicated
□ Budget allocated
□ Governance structure defined

CULTURE
□ Openness to change assessed
□ Innovation encouraged
□ Failure tolerance established
□ Learning culture promoted

TECHNOLOGY
□ Current systems inventoried
□ Integration capabilities mapped
□ Infrastructure evaluated
□ Security requirements defined

DATA
□ Data quality assessed
□ Access policies defined
□ Analytics capabilities reviewed
□ Data governance established

PROCESSES
□ Key processes documented
□ Automation opportunities identified
□ Bottlenecks mapped
□ Improvement areas prioritized

TALENT
□ Skills gap analysis completed
□ Training needs identified
□ Hiring plan developed
□ Change champions selected

CUSTOMER
□ Customer journey mapped
□ Pain points identified
□ Digital expectations assessed
□ Experience gaps analyzed

PARTNERS
□ Vendor ecosystem reviewed
□ Integration requirements defined
□ Partner capabilities assessed
□ Collaboration models evaluated

Scoring Guide:
- 64-80 points: Highly ready - Proceed with confidence
- 48-63 points: Moderately ready - Address gaps first
- 32-47 points: Limited readiness - Build foundation
- Below 32: Not ready - Significant preparation needed
