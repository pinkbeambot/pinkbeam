Workshop Facilitation Guide
============================

Complete guide for facilitating effective strategy and discovery workshops.

WORKSHOP TYPES:

1. Strategy Alignment Workshop (1/2 day)
   - Objective: Align leadership on strategic priorities
   - Participants: Executive team
   - Output: Prioritized initiative list

2. Discovery Workshop (1-2 days)
   - Objective: Understand current state and challenges
   - Participants: Cross-functional team
   - Output: Process maps, pain points, opportunities

3. Design Workshop (2-3 days)
   - Objective: Design future state solutions
   - Participants: Subject matter experts
   - Output: Solution concepts, requirements

FACILITATION TECHNIQUES:

Opening (15 mins)
□ Welcome and introductions
□ Agenda review
□ Ground rules establishment
□ Ice breaker activity

Divergent Thinking (30-45 mins)
□ Brainstorming sessions
□ Individual reflection time
□ Sticky note exercises
□ Round-robin sharing

Convergent Thinking (30-45 mins)
□ Affinity mapping
□ Prioritization matrices
□ Dot voting
□ Concept clustering

Closing (15 mins)
□ Summary of outcomes
□ Next steps review
□ Action item assignment
□ Feedback collection
